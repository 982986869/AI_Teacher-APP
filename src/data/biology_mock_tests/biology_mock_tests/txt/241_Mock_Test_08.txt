Q2. Lysosomes are produced by:
    (A) Golgi bodies   <-- CORRECT
    (B) Mitochondria
    (C) Endoplasmic reticulum
    (D) Leucoplast
    Answer: A
    Explanation: Lysosomes are manufactured and budded into the cytoplasm by the Golgi apparatus with enzymes inside. The enzymes that are within the lysosome are made in the rough endoplasmic reticulum, which is then delivered to the Golgi apparatus via transport vesicles.

    (B) A is true but R is false.   <-- CORRECT

Q2. Assertion (A): Short term adaptations have little role in evolution.
Reason (R): They are acquired.
    (A) A is false but R is true.
    (B) Both A and R are true and R is the correct explanation of A.   <-- CORRECT
    (C) Both A and R are true but R is not the correct explanation of A.
    (D) A is true but R is false.
    Answer: B
    Explanation: Short term adaptation (like defoliation of lives in autumn) are changes develop in living organisms in response to temporary environmental changes. These changes are temporary changes. Such changes are noninheritable i.e. acquired. As in these, DNA is not changed so these play no role in evolution. On the other hand long term adaptation (like type of beak in birds, modification of structures in plants) involve change in DNA hence they play an important role in evolution.

Q3. Assertion (A): Rudolf Virchow modified the hypothesis of cell theory given by Schleiden and Schwann.
Reason (R): Cell theory says that all cells arise from pre-existing cells.
    (A) A is false but R is true.
    (B) Both A and R are true and R is the correct explanation of A.
    (C) Both A and R are true but R is not the correct explanation of A.   <-- CORRECT
    (D) A is true but R is false.
    Answer: C
    Explanation: Schleiden and Schwann together formulated the cell theory. The theory, however, did not explain how new cells were formed. Rudolf Virchow (1855) first explained that cells divided and new cells are formed from pre-existing cells (Omins cellula e cellula). He modified the hypothesis of Schleiden and Schwann to give the cell theory a final shape. The present cell theory states that:
 all living organisms are composed of cells and products of cells.
 all cells arise from preexisting cells.

Their 40S subunit has an 18S RNA (1900 nucleotides) and 33 proteins.
The large subunit is composed of a 5S RNA (120 nucleotides), 28S RNA (4700 nucleotides), a 5.8S RNA (160 nucleotides) subunits, and 46 proteins.

Q12. Glycogen is a homopolymer made of :
    (A) Galactose units
    (B) Aminoacids
    (C) Ribose units
    (D) Glucose units   <-- CORRECT
    Answer: D
    Explanation: Glycogen is a multibranched polysaccharide of glucose that serves as a form of energy storage in animals, fungi, and bacteria. The polysaccharide structure represents the main storage form of glucose in the body.
