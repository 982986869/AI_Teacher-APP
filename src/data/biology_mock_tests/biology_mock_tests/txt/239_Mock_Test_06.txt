    (B) In series
    (C) Scattered
    (D) Alternately
    Answer: A

Q11. Which organelle is also known as a suicide bag of the cell?
    (A) Plastid
    (B) ER
    (C) Lysosome   <-- CORRECT
    (D) Vacuole
    Answer: C
    Explanation: Lysosomes are membrane-enclosed organelles that contain an array of enzymes capable of breaking down all types of biological polymers. One of its functions is to digest materials taken up from outside the cell. That process is called endocytosis. That's why it is called the Suicidal Bag of the cell.

Q12. Which one is an initiation codon for protein synthesis?
    (A) UGA
    (B) AUG   <-- CORRECT
    (C) UAA
    (D) UAG
    Answer: B
    Explanation: AUG codon is an initiation codon for protein synthesis. This codon code for amino acids methionine.
