Q2. Assertion (A): Homology is due to anatomical similarity and functional dissimilarity.
Reason (R): Fins of fishes and the flippers of whales are homologous organs.
    (A) Both A and R are true and R is the correct explanation of A.
    (B) A is true but R is false.   <-- CORRECT
    (C) Both A and R are true but R is not the correct explanation of A.
    (D) A is false but R is true.
    Answer: B
    Explanation: Homologous organs are those organs which have the same essential structure, but they are differently modified in adaptation to different functions. Analogous organs are those which have similar structure connected with similarity of function, have superficial resemblance to one another, but are of unlike origin. For example fins of fishes and the flippers of whales serve the same function and hence are analogous.
