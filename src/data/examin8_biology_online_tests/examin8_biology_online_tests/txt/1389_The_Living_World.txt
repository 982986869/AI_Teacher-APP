Q13. A specified area in which multiple uses of the land is permitted by dividing into zones, each for a particular activity is called ________.
    (A) Wildlife reserves
    (B) Zoological park
    (C) Biosphere reserve   <-- CORRECT
    (D) Museums
    Answer: C
    Explanation: A biosphere reserve is divided into a number of zones, where different kinds of activities are allowed. In the outer zone, agriculture and other activities are allowed but in the central or core zone, no human activities are allowed.
