Q1. Which of the following blood components play a major role in blood coagulation?
    (A) Lymphocytes
    (B) Thrombocytes   <-- CORRECT
    (C) Fibrinocytes
    (D) Granulocytes
    Answer: B
    Explanation: Platelets also called thrombocytes are cell fragments produced from megakaryocytes. Platelets can release a variety of substances most of which are involved in the coagulation or clotting of blood.

Q1. The specialized patch of modified heart muscles from where contraction initiates, is/are:
    (A) Pacemaker of heart   <-- CORRECT

    (C) 70 mm Hg

    Answer: A
