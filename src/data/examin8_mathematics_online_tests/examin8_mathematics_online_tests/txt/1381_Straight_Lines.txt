    (A) - 2
    (B) 8   <-- CORRECT
    (C) - 8
    (D) 2
    Answer: B

$ \text { Distance }=\frac{\left|C_2-C_1\right|}{\sqrt{A^2+B^2}} $
For the lines $5 x-12 y+65=0$ and $5 x-12 y-39=0$ : - Here, $A=5, B=-12, C_1=65$, and $C_2=-39$. Now, we calculate the distance:
$ \text { Distance }=\frac{|-39-65|}{\sqrt{5^2+(-12)^2}}=\frac{|-104|}{\sqrt{25+144}}=\frac{104}{\sqrt{169}}=\frac{104}{13}=8 $
