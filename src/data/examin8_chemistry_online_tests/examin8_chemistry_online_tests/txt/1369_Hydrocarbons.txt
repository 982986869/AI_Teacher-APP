Q2. Saturated hydrocarbons contain

    (C) ionic bonds
    (D) double bonds

    Explanation: Saturated hydrocarbons are the compounds in which there is a single bond between the carbon atoms. They are also called alkanes and have the general formula C_nH_2n+2.

Q7. The number of possible conformational isomers of ethane is ________.
    (A) zero

Q10. The general formula for alkenes is:

    (C) C_nH_2n-1

    Explanation: Alkenes are unsaturated hydrocarbons with at least on double bond between the carbon atoms. Their general formula is C_nH_2n.

Q11. Carbon-carbon double bond in alkenes consists of:
    (A) one strong sigma $\left( \sigma \right)$ bond due to head-on overlapping of sp^2 hybridised orbitals one weak pi $\left( \pi \right)$ bond.   <-- CORRECT

The bond angle in cyclobutane is = 90^o
The normal tetrahedral bond angle
= 109.5^o
$\therefore$ Deviation = (normal tetrahedral bond angle - actual normal bond angle)
= (109.5^o - 90^o)
= 19.5^o
Hence, the bond angle deviation for cyclobutane is 19.5^o.
